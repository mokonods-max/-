✦ Our Heritage ✦

Born from the
Soul of Baghdad

Rose Baghdad was founded in 2022 with a singular mission — to bring the ancient art of Oriental perfumery to those who seek more than just a scent. We seek to evoke memory, emotion, and identity.

Each fragrance is a journey — through the rose gardens of Baghdad, the souks of antiquity, and the timeless tradition of Arabian incense culture. Every bottle tells a story.
